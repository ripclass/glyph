> v1.0.0 | Last updated: 2026-04-04 | Owner: KhaM Health

## Purpose

Reference list of commonly prescribed drugs in Bangladesh, organized by therapeutic class. Includes local brand names, generic names, common dosage forms, and strengths. Used by extraction prompts for brand-to-generic mapping and by clinical prompts for drug interaction checking and cost-awareness.

## Important Notes

1. **Brand names vary by manufacturer.** Bangladesh has 300+ pharmaceutical companies. The same generic may have 20+ brand names. This list covers the most commonly encountered brands.
2. **Pricing context.** Most drugs are very affordable in BD due to domestic manufacturing. A course of antibiotics may cost 50-200 BDT ($0.50-$2.00). Newer drugs (SGLT2 inhibitors, DOACs) are more expensive.
3. **OTC availability.** Most drugs (including antibiotics) are available without prescription at pharmacies in BD, despite regulations.
4. **Drug quality.** Major manufacturers (Square, Beximco, Incepta, Renata, ACI, Eskayef, Opsonin, Aristopharma) are generally reliable and many are WHO-prequalified.
5. **This list is not exhaustive.** It covers the most commonly prescribed drugs across major therapeutic classes. Specialty drugs, oncology drugs, and rare medications are not included.
