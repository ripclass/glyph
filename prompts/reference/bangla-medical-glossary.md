# Bangla Medical Glossary
> v1.0.0 | Last updated: 2026-04-04 | Owner: KhaM Health

## Purpose

Reference glossary of common medical terms in Bangla with English mappings. Used by all prompts to ensure consistent terminology. Organized by clinical category. Includes both formal Bangla and colloquial terms patients actually use.

## Usage Notes

1. **Colloquial terms are more important than standard terms** for patient-facing prompts. Patients say "গ্যাস্ট্রিক" not "পেপটিক আলসার" and "সুগার" not "ডায়াবেটিস মেলিটাস".
2. **English medical terms used in BD practice**: BP, diabetes, CBC, ECG, X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan, MRI, HIV, ICU -- these are used directly in Bangla conversation and should not be translated.
3. **Regional variations exist.** Chittagonian, Sylheti, and other dialects may use different terms. This glossary covers standard Bangla and common colloquial usage.
4. **New terms**: "Corona" / "কোভিড" is widely understood. "অক্সিমিটার" (oximeter) has entered common vocabulary.
